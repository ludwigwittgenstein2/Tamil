\subsection{About this work}\label{about}

This essay explores the historical roots, lived experiences, and
proposed remedies for caste‑based discrimination in modern Tamil~Nadu
from Tirunelveli. It can be part of Sociology, Anthropology work on
social mobility, hierarchy in South India.

\subsection{References:}\label{references}

B.R. Ambedkar's \emph{Annihilation of Caste} remains a critical text for
understanding caste in India and Tamil Nadu. Originally written in 1936
as a speech and later self-published, it critiques the caste system and
advocates for its abolition.\footnote{B.R. Ambedkar, \emph{Annihilation
  of Caste}, 1936. Available:
  https://ia601009.us.archive.org/20/items/AnnihilationOfCasteDr.B.r.ambedkar/Annihilation\%20of\%20caste\%20Dr.B.r.ambedkar.pdf}
